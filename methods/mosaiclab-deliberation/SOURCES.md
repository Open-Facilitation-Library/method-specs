## What was adapted, and what was scoped out
The six stages encode the in-room deliberative conversation: MosaicLab's Steps 4-6 (processing information, dialogue and deliberation, decision-making), with a light front stage drawn from Steps 1-2 (the remit plus the group's own agreements). The surrounding assembly program is out of scope: recruitment and stratified selection, the sponsor's remit-setting and public promise, sourcing expert speakers and site visits, skill-development training (Step 3), and report formatting and the presentation to decision-makers (Step 7). Those are program design, not facilitated conversation; the chain assumes a remit and balanced information exist and hands its recommendations back to that program.
