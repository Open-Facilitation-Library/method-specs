## What was adapted
The five stages encode the sensemaking sequence the M2M case studies actually ran (especially Opus / River Dôn: asset mapping, role mapping, risk mapping under a "governance follows readiness" loop). The contracting / legal-architecture work that M2M culminates in is deliberately **out of scope**: it is legal co-design, not conversation facilitation. This spec hands off to it.
